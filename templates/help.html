Help & Documentation

Getting Started

1. Upload Your Documents

Start by uploading your documents using the "Upload Document" button in the documents panel. The system supports PDF, TXT, MD, and DOCX formats. The system will process your document and prepare it for question answering.

2. Ask Questions

Type your question in the input field and click "Ask" or press Enter. The AI will analyze your documents and provide relevant answers based on the content.

3. Review Answers and Sources

The AI will provide an answer along with the sources it used from your documents. You can review these sources to verify the information.

Features

  • RAG Architecture: Uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation for accurate, document-based answers.
  • Llama 3.1 Model: Powered by Meta's Llama 3.1 for high-quality responses.
  • Document Management: Easy upload and management of multiple document types.
  • Conversation History: Maintains context across multiple questions for more coherent interactions.
  • Vector Search: Uses semantic search to find the most relevant information in your documents.
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices.

Tips & Tricks

  • Be Specific: Ask clear, focused questions for better results.
  • Context Matters: The AI can only answer based on the content of your uploaded documents.
  • Follow-up Questions: The system remembers previous questions, so you can ask follow-ups without repeating context.
  • Review Sources: Always check the source information to verify the AI's answers.
  • Toggle Panels: Use the side buttons to hide document and history panels for more space on smaller screens.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Document won't upload
Ensure your document is not password protected and is in a supported format (PDF, TXT, MD, DOCX).
Slow responses
Large documents may take longer to process. The first query after uploading a document might be slower as the system indexes the content.
Unclear or incorrect answers
Try rephrasing your question or being more specific. Check if the information you're looking for is actually in your documents.
System not starting
Make sure Docker is running for the vector database (Qdrant) and that you have the required model files in the models directory.
